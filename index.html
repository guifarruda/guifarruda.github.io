Biography

I am a researcher at Universidade de São Paulo. My interesests are non-linear dynamics on top of complex structured populations. More specifically I am focusing in epidemic and rumor spreading in single and multilayer networks. Additionally, stuctural characterization of multilayer networks, mainly by means of spectral theory, is one of the areas I am concerned of. If you need further information, please don't hesitate to contact me.
